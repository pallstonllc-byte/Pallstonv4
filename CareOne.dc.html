The Problem

Administration is fragmented across systems that don't talk to each other

Healthcare administration remains fragmented across disconnected EHRs, payer portals, clearinghouses, phone calls, faxes, PDFs, spreadsheets, and manual reviews. This creates delayed care, preventable denials, slower reimbursement, excessive administrative cost, and limited visibility across the full reimbursement lifecycle.

How It Works

One intelligent workflow, from eligibility to reimbursement

Six coordinated stages run on a shared intelligence layer — each step passes explainable, auditable context to the next.

STEP 1
Verify

Eligibility & Benefits Intelligence

Instantly verifies patient coverage, active policy status, deductibles, copays, exclusions, and benefit rules before care is delivered.

STEP 2
Authorize

Prior Authorization Automation

Reviews diagnosis codes, clinical documentation, payer policies, and medical necessity criteria to approve, deny, or escalate requests.

STEP 3
Document

Clinical Documentation Review

Identifies missing documentation, summarizes clinical context, and ensures the record supports the requested service or claim.

STEP 4
Validate

Claim & Coding Validation

Checks CPT, ICD-10, modifiers, charges, payer rules, and documentation alignment before submission.

STEP 5
Protect

Payment Integrity & Fraud Detection

Flags duplicate billing, upcoding, unbundling, excessive units, fee schedule anomalies, and suspicious patterns before payment.

STEP 6
Resolve

Denials, Appeals & Payment Orchestration

Generates appeal packages, explains denial reasons, tracks claim status, and supports final payment reconciliation.

The Reasoning Engine

How Claude powers the platform

Claude serves as the reasoning engine behind the platform. It reads and reasons across physician notes, payer policies, medical necessity criteria, contracts, denial letters, coding rules, authorization requirements, invoices, and claim histories.

Rather than acting as a chatbot, Claude powers specialized AI agents that coordinate administrative decisions with explainable, auditable recommendations.

FIRST 90-DAY MVP

Prior Authorization + Claim Validation

The first release focuses on two of healthcare's most painful administrative bottlenecks: prior authorization and pre-submission claim validation. Providers submit authorization requests or claims through existing workflows while the platform verifies eligibility, reviews payer requirements, checks documentation, validates coding, predicts denial risk, and recommends the next best action.

This creates fast ROI by reducing manual review, minimizing preventable denials, accelerating reimbursement, and giving revenue cycle teams a single AI workspace for every claim.
